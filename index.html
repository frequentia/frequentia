About Our Association

Frequentia, the dynamic statistics association of St. Joseph's University. At Frequentia, we believe that behind every data point lies a story waiting to be uncovered. Our mission is to foster a community of statistical enthusiasts who are passionate about exploring these stories, unraveling patterns, and making meaningful contributions to a data-driven world.

Events Conducted

Frequentia 2023-24 Inauguration

As the curtain rises on the impending inauguration of our Statistics Association, excitement fills the air. Adaptus Statistica

Frequentia's Adaptus Statistica buzzed with dynamic competitions, enlightening workshops, and valuable networking. A celebration of statistical excellence, it united data enthusiasts, emphasizing the field's importance across campuses and highlighting the Association's commitment to fostering knowledge and collaboration.

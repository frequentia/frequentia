About Our Association

Frequentia, the dynamic statistics association of St. Joseph's University. At Frequentia, we believe that behind every data point lies a story waiting to be uncovered. Our mission is to foster a community of statistical enthusiasts who are passionate about exploring these stories, unraveling patterns, and making meaningful contributions to a data-driven world.
